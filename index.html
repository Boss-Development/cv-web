Welcome to Wicked RP!

The official website for the Wicked Rp Gaming Community!

Who We Are

Wicked RP is a gaming community started by 2 close friends. Wanting a place to hang out an play some games together, Sharky and Jolk started a FiveM server. Sharky’s knowledge in IT and server hosting & management and Jolk’s knowledge in media creation helped make the community what it is today.

The 2 started on the FiveM platform for GTA V but have plans to expand to other games in the future, such as Space Engineers, Satisfactory, American Truck Simulator, and much more.

What We Provide

We currently only offer a FiveM RP server but plan to branch out to more games down the line. Feel free to check out our servers.

FiveM - Wicked Life RP

Built on the latest version of the vRP framework, we offer a server that focus on realism. You can live the life of a normal Civilian with the multiple jobs to choose from, or live the life as a Criminal and build an underground empire. There are also opportunities to become a Law Enforcement Officer, Firefighter, Paramedic, or even a Emergency Dispatcher.

What We Play

We currently play multiple games with the community. These games include, but are not limited to, FiveM, Sea of Thieves, American Truck Sim, ARK: Survival Evolved, and Farming Sim 19. We have dedicated groups/companies for FiveM and ATS with opportunities to manage your own group inside of the Wicked RP Gaming Community.

American Trucking Simulator - Wicked Logistics

Managed via TrucksBooks, Wicked Logistics was started at the beginning of July 2021. We offer a wide range of fun from living the life as a Truck Driver to the clerical life of a Truck Company manger. To find out more about the Wicked Logistics Company, please join our discord server.

Sea of Thieves

Set sail with your crew and take on the seas of Pirates. Live your life as a pirate on and adventure to search for the worlds’ riches and secrets. Join our discord server to learn more about our adventures.

ARK: Survival Evolved

Start your life in prehistoric times. Build your way up to the age of technology. Work together with your friends or try your hand at lone survival.
